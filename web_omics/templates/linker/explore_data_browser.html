The provided omics data are shown here in different data tables. Clicking an entity in one table will display connected entities in other tables and additional information for that entity in the bottom panel. A blue circle next to the entity name indicates an observed entity (has a measurement in the uploaded data) while a white circle next to the entity name indicates an inferred entity (connected according to Reactome database). To create a comparison between different experimental conditions, please go to the Inference tab. The resulting comparison will be shown as a column in the data table, and entries are colour-coded according to the fold change. Highlighting a mouse will display the p-value.
